Complete Blood Count (CBC)

Collection & Critical Action
  • Tube: EDTA (Purple‑top)
  • Volume: 2‑4 mL whole blood
  • Site: Venous puncture
  • Critical values: Hb <7 g/dL or >20 g/dL, WBC <1 or >30 ×10³/µL, Platelets <20 or >1000 ×10³/µL
  • Action: Notify physician within 30 min; prepare for transfusion if Hb critically low.

Renal Function Test

Collection & Critical Action
  • Tube: Serum Separator (Gold‑top) or Lithium Heparin (Green‑top)
  • Volume: 2‑3 mL serum/plasma
  • Site: Venous
  • Critical values: Creatinine >6 mg/dL, BUN >100 mg/dL
  • Action: Rapid nephrology consult; assess for dialysis need.

Electrolyte Panel

Collection & Critical Action
  • Tube: Serum Separator (Gold) or Lithium Heparin (Green)
  • Volume: 2‑3 mL serum/plasma
  • Site: Venous (Arterial for ABG if required)
  • Critical values: Na <120 or >160; K <2.5 or >6.5; Cl <80 or >115; CO₂ <10 or >40
  • Action: Stat electrolyte replacement or ICU transfer as indicated.

Coagulation Profile

Collection & Critical Action
  • Tube: Citrate (Blue‑top)
  • Volume: 2.7 mL whole blood (9:1 blood:citrate)
  • Site: Venous
  • Critical values: PT >50 s, aPTT >100 s
  • Action: Hold anticoagulants; administer FFP/Vit K if indicated; urgent hematology consult.

International Normalized Ratio (INR)

Collection & Critical Action
  • Tube: Citrate (Blue‑top)
  • Volume: 2.7 mL
  • Site: Venous
  • Critical values: INR >5 (non‑therapeutic)
  • Action: Reverse warfarin with Vit K/FFP; monitor for bleeding.

Liver Function Test

Collection & Critical Action
  • Tube: Serum Separator (Gold)
  • Volume: 2‑3 mL serum
  • Site: Venous
  • Critical values: ALT/AST >1000 U/L, Total Bilirubin >20 mg/dL
  • Action: Rule out acute liver failure; prepare for transfer to transplant center.

Thyroid Function Test

Collection & Critical Action
  • Tube: Serum Separator (Gold)
  • Volume: 2‑3 mL serum
  • Site: Venous
  • Critical values: TSH <0.1 or >30 mU/L
  • Action: If thyroid storm/myxedema coma suspected, initiate ICU care.

Cardiac Enzymes

Collection & Critical Action
  • Tube: Serum Separator (Gold) or Lithium Heparin (Green)
  • Volume: 3 mL serum/plasma
  • Site: Venous
  • Critical values: Troponin I >0.4 ng/mL, CK‑MB >10 ng/mL
  • Action: Activate chest‑pain pathway; immediate cardiology consult & serial enzymes.
